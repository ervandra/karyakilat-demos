# Karya Kilat Demo Project - PRD

## Overview
Create 4 high-quality Next.js microsites to demonstrate the "Karya Kilat" (Instant Creation) value proposition to different professional verticals in Indonesia.

## Tech Stack (All Demos)
- **Framework:** Next.js 14+ (App Router)
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
- **Icons:** Lucide React
- **Images:** Nano Banana Pro (AI Generated) or placeholders
- **Language:** Bahasa Indonesia (Professional & Persuasive)

---

## Demo 1: Senior Insurance Agent (Trust & Legacy)
**Target:** Senior agents selling high-value life/legacy products.
**Path:** `./demo1`
**Theme:** "Trust, Legacy, Protection"
**Color Palette:** Deep Blue (#1e3a8a), Gold (#d97706), White. Serif fonts for headings.
**Pain Points:**
- Hard to explain complex policies quickly.
- Clients trust the *person*, not just the PDF brochure.
- Needs to look established and premium.
**Key Sections:**
1.  **Hero:** Professional headshot (placeholder), tagline "Melindungi Warisan Keluarga Anda".
2.  **About Me:** 15+ years experience, MDRT member.
3.  **Services:** Legacy Planning, Critical Illness, Education Fund.
4.  **Testimonials:** "Pak Budi helped my family..."
5.  **CTA:** WhatsApp for consultation.

## Demo 2: Property Agent (Luxury & Visuals)
**Target:** Agents selling high-end apartments/houses (PIK, South Jakarta).
**Path:** `./demo2`
**Theme:** "Visuals, Luxury, Closing"
**Color Palette:** Black, Dark Grey, White, minimal accent. Modern Sans-serif.
**Pain Points:**
- Listings get lost in portals.
- Needs to showcase high-res photos without clutter.
- Fast contact for viewing is critical.
**Key Sections:**
1.  **Hero:** Full-screen luxury interior image, "Hunian Eksklusif di Jantung Jakarta".
2.  **Featured Listings:** Carousel or grid of 3 top properties (Price, Location, Specs).
3.  **Why Buy Now:** Investment value, location analysis.
4.  **CTA:** "Jadwalkan Private Viewing".

## Demo 3: Professional Tax Consultant (Authority & Savings)
**Target:** Tax planners helping SMEs and freelancers.
**Path:** `./demo3`
**Theme:** "Authority, Compliance, Savings"
**Color Palette:** Teal/Emerald (#047857), Clean White, Light Grey. Trustworthy & Calm.
**Pain Points:**
- Clients fear audits.
- Tax laws are confusing.
- Needs to prove expertise without overwhelming jargon.
**Key Sections:**
1.  **Hero:** Clean, professional look. "Urus Pajak Tanpa Was-was".
2.  **Problems We Solve:** SPT Tahunan, Tax Amnesty, Corporate Tax.
3.  **Process:** Simple 3-step workflow (Consult -> Audit -> File).
4.  **FAQ:** Common tax questions.
5.  **CTA:** "Konsultasi Gratis 15 Menit".
